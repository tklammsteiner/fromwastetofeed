Working Group & Management Committee Meeting Innsbruck

Information

Meeting venue

The Meeting will take place on the 29 & 30 August 2025 at the Center for Chemistry and Biomedicine (Innrain 80, 6020 Innsbruck, Austria, GoogleMaps). The large lecture hall (L.EG.220) is located at the ground floor of the building, with the foyer hosting the coffee breaks and poster session right in front of it. The smaller seminar room for the parallel session (L.01.220) is located on the first floor.

Coffee breaks & lunch

Coffee breaks will be held in the foyer on the ground floor. On Friday, we will take a short (5-minute) walk together to the UNI Lounge canteen in a nearby building for lunch. You can view the menu here. While coffee and snacks during the breaks are provided by the meeting organizers, lunch expenses must be covered individually using the daily allowance.

Social Dinner

The social dinner will take place on Friday, 29 August, starting at 19:30 at Gasthof Bierstindl. Dinner expenses must be covered individually using the daily allowance.

Posters

To foster collaboration and identify shared research interests among COST Action members, we invite you to prepare a poster (A0 vertical format) introducing your working group or lab and summarizing your general research focus. The poster sessions are meant as an opportunity to familiarize participants with each other’s research groups rather than to present recent findings.


To facilitate planning for the social dinner and poster sessions, please register here [HERE] until July 31st.
