A new peer-reviewed conference paper as a summary of our results from our citizen science project has been published in Proceedings of Science as part of the 5th Austrian Citizen Science Conference that took place in July 2019 in Obergurgl, Austria. The paper is accessible via an open access license at [https://pos.sissa.it/366/006](https://pos.sissa.it/366/006)


**Publication**:
  
**On everyone’s lips: insects for food and feed**. Klammsteiner, T., Walter, A., Pan, H., Gassner, M., Heussler, C.D., Schermer, M., Insam, H., 2020. *PoS(ACSC2019)* 336.
[https://doi.org/10.22323/1.366.0006](https://doi.org/10.22323/1.366.0006)
