A new article focussing on the use of Black Soldier Fly frass as soil amendment and its implication for organic waste hygienization has been published as part of the Research Topic "*Smart fertilizers and innovative organic amendments for sustainable agricultural systems*" in Agronomy. The paper is accessible via an open access license at [https://www.mdpi.com/2073-4395/10/10/1578](https://www.mdpi.com/2073-4395/10/10/1578)

**Suitability of black soldier fly frass as soil amendment and implication for organic waste hygienization**. Klammsteiner, T., Turan, V., Fernández-Delgado Juárez, M., Oberegger, S., Insam, H., 2020. *Agronomy* 10(10), 1578.
[https://doi.org/10.3389/fmicb.2020.00993](https://doi.org/10.3389/fmicb.2020.00993)
